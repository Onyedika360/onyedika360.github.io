About Me

I am a Computer Science student with a passion for machine learning and artificial intelligence. My journey in technology started with a curiosity about how things work. Initially, I intended to delve into aerospace, but I soon discovered my passion for data science as I began working on various projects.
My most recent project involves researching and building a classification model for neurodegenerative diseases using time series datasets and machine learning techniques and technologies. I am the founding president of the Data Science Club at Texas Southern University, fostering collaborations with companies like Google, Oracle, and others.
In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I am also a small business owner, running a food trucking business.
